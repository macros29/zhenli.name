Hello!

My name is Zhen Li. I am a Postdoc working in Dr. Joseph G. Gleeson’s laboratory, at University of California, San Diego. I use single cell genomics technologies to study brain development and diseases. I completed my PhD training at Yale University, under the supervision of Dr. Nenad Sestan. I will periodically update this website with information I found useful or interesting. Please stop by!
